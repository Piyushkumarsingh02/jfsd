About Us

At BeyondGrade, we believe in empowering students to document and showcase their extracurricular achievements. Our platform is designed to help you keep track of your skills, activities, and milestones, all in one place.

We understand the importance of recognizing achievements beyond academic grades. That's why we provide an intuitive interface that allows you to easily log your accomplishments, from sports and arts to volunteer work and internships.

Join us in celebrating your talents and let BeyondGrade be your partner in growth and success!
